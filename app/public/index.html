🔬 What Makes This App Different

Most healthcare dashboards show you a number and say "trust this." We don't.
This app reads the raw text of each facility's description, capability, procedure, and equipment fields. It cross-references claims against structural data — and shows you cited text evidence so you can verify every claim yourself.
